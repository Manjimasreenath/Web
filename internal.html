THRISSUR

Thrissur District can claim the honour of having been in the forefront of the country-wide movement for temple entry and abolition of untouchability.
The famous Guruvayur Satyagraha is a memorable episode in the history of the national movement.

Places to visit

  • guruvayoor temple
  • kauthuka park
